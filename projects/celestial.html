Celestial

Shining amongst the bright stars

Celestial, a women's fashion brand based in South Africa, aspires to empower women with confidence through elegant and mature clothing. Their tagline, "Amongst the Stars," reflects their mission.

Overview

The Journey into the night sky

The brand needed to redefine itself to better connect with its target demographic of mature, confident women. The existing brand image did not convey the desired essence of elegance and confidence, and the challenge was to create a new visual identity and brand experience.

The goal was to empower women to feel confident and self-assured through their clothing choices with a sense of understated sophistication. The design had to strike a balance between boldness and subtlety, aligning with Celestial's core values and resonating with its audience.

Discovery & Strategy

Preparing to Fly to the Galaxy

During this phase, I had detailed conversations with Celestial to comprehend their vision and challenges. We examined the core of what it means to exude confidence and elegance as a woman. Furthermore, we conducted user and market research to gain a better understanding of the fashion preferences and aspirations of our target audience.

Based on our research findings, we formulated a strategy that emphasized a simple yet bold design concept. Our goal was to create a brand that resonated with mature women seeking confidence in their attire. We collaborated with the Celestial team to align our design approach with their mission and values.

Design & Development

Bringing the Stars to Life

After careful planning and refinement, we developed a winning strategy that makes a lasting impact. We created a visual identity that embodies Celestial's brand and sets it apart. It was challenging but exciting, and the result exceeded our expectations.

The logo design featured a minimalistic type form of "Celestial" with a star replacing the dot on the 'i.'

Incorporated simple shapes like a quarter moon, stars, and sun to create brand assets that reinforced the celestial theme.

Utilized bold colors in contrast with pastels to evoke a sense of confidence and elegance.

Results

Brand in the galaxy

The rebranding of Celestial successfully repositioned the brand in the market, resonating with the target audience of mature and confident women.

User engagement increased by 13.5% within the first quarter after the new brand launch.

Customer feedback overwhelmingly praised the brand's new image, emphasizing feelings of confidence and elegance.
